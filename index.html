MARILOU QUINTO

A selfless mother whose love is endless

A mother of three, Mrs. Marilou Quinto unconditionally revolves her life around her children.

About

My mother is no doubt one of the most loving, reliable, and thoughtful person you'll ever get to know. Anyone could say that she has this engaging aura that draws you to her kindness with ease. With that, she is well appreciated and greatly respected by every person around her, without anything to say against her. She would say that her children are her life, and that she would give anything for them to lead a fulfilling one. Moreover, she is very wise and clever when it comes to businesses, and she has the capabilities that one could not help but be in awe.

A Brief Timeline

1972 - She was born in San Jose, Bulacan

1990 - She married my father, Mr. Orlando Quinto

1991 - She gave birth to her first child

1996 - She gave birth to her second child

2000 - She and her family settled in Pangasinan for good

2004 - She gave birth to her third and last child, which is me!

2022. Marilou Quinto and her family during her first son's wedding.

A Message to Her

In this world, you are my world. You are eternally beautiful and kind. If I know what love is, it is because of you. As your daughter, all that I am and who I hope to be, I owe it to you—my mother.

"I can imagine no heroism greater than motherhood."
